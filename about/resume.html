About

Fugang He (何伏刚)

Fugang He received his PhD at Beijing Normal university. He is at present an Associate Professor at the Department of police command and tactics, in the College of police training, People’s Public Security University of China. Dr. He’s research focuses on the use of information systems for police training, police command and Criminal law enforcement. He has papers in Modern Education Technolegy, Journal of Distance education and distance education of China. He served as researcher in RCDE. He is also the head of police command.
